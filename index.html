Branching Out:

2/11/2021

The year 2020 proved itself to be quite an arduous one for me. Unlike many others who were out of work, I was not. I worked more hours than I have ever before during the raging pandemic. Financially, the Covid 19 pandemic has been great for me though, at the cost of many peoples lives and livelihoods, however. As the world seems to have changed from what it once was a year ago, I have changed for the better with it, and have a new, optimistic outlook for the future. I mean it can't get much worse, right? Anyways, part of this future involves changing careers, so I don't have to grind my body into the ground anymore to make a living. I have grown weary of the coorporate retail life, and would like to progress into the exciting and ever changing world of coding and beyond!
